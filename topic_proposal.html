Topic Description

In the fields of education and learning, significant advances have emerged from the increasing use of educational technology. This technology serves as tools to enhance the educational experience at each academic level which traditional methods can no longer successfully address. Technology is instrumental to today’s educational system, and thanks to the development and integration of online learning, students can unlock new opportunities. Online learning has caused an increase in adaptive learning systems which “allows for the creation of a personalized learning experience for students where each learner can complete a course differently”.​
